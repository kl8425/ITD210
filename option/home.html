Different Cheeses

Parmesan: Hard cheese that is often aged for long periods of time. Has a fruity and nutty flavor.

Cheddar: Lots of different variations, flavor ranges, and colors. Popular cheese for grilled cheeses.

Mozzarella: Very stretchy and melts well that is often used on pizza. Normally a white color.

Brie: Soft and white that comes in a wheel. Works well as a dessert cheese.
